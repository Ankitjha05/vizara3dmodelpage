Hampi Stone Chariot

It is a famous Stone Chariot of Hampi in Karnataka, a UNESCO World heritage site in India.Stone Chariot is located inside the premises of the Vijaya Vittala temple at Hampi. Lord Vittala is an avatar of Indian god Vishnu, and this chariot is a shrine of Lord Garuda (a mythological figure with a human body and head of a large, legendary bird), who is the ‘Vahana’ or vehicle of Lord Vishnu.
